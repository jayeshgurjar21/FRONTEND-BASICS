Live course will happen for 4 months but you will have course access for 1 year from date of admission.

You can ask doubts in the community section where other students and mentors will answer. Also live classes can use used to ask the doubts directly to Prashant Sir.

Yes, you will receive a certificate of completion after the course finishes.

Is there a schedule for the classes?

Yes, there will be a live class of 90 minutes on every weekday(Mon-Fri)
